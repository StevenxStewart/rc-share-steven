Visionaries Unbound

FOUR EVENTS BRINGING TOGETHER GLOBAL LEADERS FOR INNOVATIVE URBAN, ENVIRONMENTAL, HEALTH AND LIVELIHOOD SOLUTIONS.

Carolyn Whelan - 2013

After unheralded growth, the world is bumping up against its limits. Widespread affluence and urbanization have enriched many. But the rising tide also strains resources-the food and water that nourish our farms and families; the land on which our homes and cities sits; the road and sky byways for delivery of goods and ideas; the trees and roots that help keep our air and water pure.

We face an inflection point - and a brilliant opportunity to shift that trajectory. Medical breakthroughs, sustainable building materials, evolving micro-financing models, exxpanding data resources, and innovative ways of working, communicating and governing are a few advances that can bring transformational change- If linked and leveraged.

The “Visionaries Unbound” event series aims to do just that. Four events focusing on cities, ecosystems, healthcare and livelihoods will spark new ideas and action by cross-pollinating expert insight. Leaders in business, government, academia and non-governmental orgnizations will gather to identify innovative solutions for a more resilient, equitable and socially inclusive world. Proposals will be grounded in real trends and projections, address obstacles and be future-oriented and actionable.

Please join us online on this journey.

The series is funded and hosted by the Rockefeller Foundation, and is organised with participation of The Economist Interlligence Unit.
